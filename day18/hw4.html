Sure, here's a simple recipe for homemade pizza in a sorted list: 1. **Ingredients:** - Pizza dough - Pizza sauce - Mozzarella cheese, shredded - Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives) 2. **Instructions:** a. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). b. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. c. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. d. Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. e. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. f. Add your desired toppings on top of the cheese. g. Optional: sprinkle additional cheese on top of the toppings for extra cheesiness. h. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted. i. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
